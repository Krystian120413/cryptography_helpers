Where is Maldives located?
The Maldives is an archipelago located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of Sri Lanka and India. It consists of 26 atolls, which are made up of more than 1,000 coral islands.
Do we need passport for Maldives?
Yes, you need a valid passport to enter the Maldives. Your passport should have at least 6 months of validity remaining from the date of entry.
Is Maldives a muslim country?
The Maldives is officially an Islamic country, and Islam is the predominant religion practiced by the majority of the population.

What is the capital of Maldives?
The capital city of the Maldives is Malé. It is one of the most densely populated cities in the world and serves as the political, economic, and cultural hub of the country.
Is Maldives open for indian tourists?
Yes, the Maldives is open for Indian tourists. Indian nationals are among the largest groups of tourists visiting the Maldives each year.
Where is Maldives located in which country?
The Maldives is an independent country and not located within any other country. It is situated southwest of India, stretching across the equator.
Do indians need visa for maldives?
No, Indian passport holders do not require a pre-arrival visa for the Maldives. They are granted a free visa-on-arrival for stays of up to 90 days.
Is Maldives open for tourism?
Yes, the Maldives is open for tourism. The Maldivian government has been actively promoting tourism, and resorts have been implementing safety measures to ensure the well-being of visitors amidst the COVID-19 pandemic.
How many islands in Maldives?
The Maldives is composed of approximately 1,200 islands, which are grouped into 26 atolls. These islands are renowned for their pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life.

Which is the best time to visit Maldives
The best time to visit the Maldives is generally from November to April, during the dry northeast monsoon season. This period offers sunny weather, calm seas, and excellent visibility for snorkeling, diving, and other water sports.
Who is the current president of Maldives?
As of my last update, the President of the Maldives was Ibrahim Mohamed Solih. He assumed office in November 2018 after winning the presidential election.
How many airports in Maldives?
The Maldives has several airports, including Velana International Airport (MLE) in Hulhulé Island, which serves as the main gateway for international flights.
Which currency is used in Maldives?
The official currency of the Maldives is the Maldivian Rufiyaa (MVR). However, US dollars are widely accepted in resorts, hotels, and tourist areas.
How many days are enough for Maldives?
The ideal duration for a visit to the Maldives depends on individual preferences and interests. While some travelers opt for short getaways of 3-5 days, others may choose to stay for a week or more to fully experience the beauty and tranquility of the islands.
